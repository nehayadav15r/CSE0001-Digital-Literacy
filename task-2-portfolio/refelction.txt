
Reflection: Stepping into the Professional World

As a student, setting up my GitHub and LinkedIn felt like "leveling up." 
At first, it was a bit intimidating to look at a blank profile and wonder if I had enough to show. 
But as I started adding my technical skills and documenting my projects, I realized I’ve actually built a solid foundation.

This process was more than just a task; it was about shifting my mindset from just "doing homework" to building a career. 
Seeing my work organized in a clean repository gave me a real sense of ownership and confidence. 
It’s a great feeling to know that my digital footprint now actually reflects the hard work I’m putting into my degree.
